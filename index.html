Vacations in Maldives

$1660 - 5 Days

"Escape to the Maldives: Where Turquoise Dreams and Sun-Kissed Shores Await!"

Experience the ultimate 5-day escape to the Maldives, a paradise of turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and breathtaking sunsets. Stay in luxurious overwater villas, enjoy gourmet dining, and unwind with soothing spa treatments. Dive into vibrant coral reefs, embark on thrilling water sports, or take a serene sunset cruise. Whether you seek adventure or tranquility, the Maldives promises an unforgettable journey of relaxation and beauty. Book now to create memories that last a lifetime!

Day 1: Arrival in the Maldives

-Arrive at Velana International Airport and transfer to your luxurious resort via speedboat or seaplane.
-Spend the day settling into your overwater villa and enjoying the stunning views.
-Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 2: Relaxation & Water Activities

-Start your day with a sunrise yoga session or a relaxing spa treatment.
-Explore the crystal-clear lagoons with snorkeling or paddleboarding.
-In the evening, enjoy a romantic beachfront dinner.
-Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 3: Island Exploration & Dolphin Cruise

-Take a guided tour to a nearby local island to experience Maldivian culture.
-In the afternoon, embark on a dolphin-watching cruise and witness a magical sunset over the Indian Ocean.
-Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 4: Adventure & Underwater Wonders

-Dive into vibrant coral reefs or opt for thrilling water sports like jet skiing and parasailing.
-Relax by the infinity pool or indulge in a sunset cocktail. Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 5: Departure from the Maldives

-Spend your final morning soaking up the sun on the pristine beach.
-Transfer to the airport for your departure, leaving with unforgettable memories of paradise.
